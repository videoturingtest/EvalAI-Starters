As described in the research paper with respect to the video understanding, we define the evaulation metrics as follows:

Shot-level performance: Shot-level QA performance on the DramaQA dataset. The questions for this level are based on a video length less than about 10 seconds without change of viewpoint.

Scene-level performance: Scene-level QA performance on the DramaQA dataset. The set of questions for this level is based on clips that are 1-3 minutes long without place change.

Overall performance: QA performance on the entire DramaQA dataset.
